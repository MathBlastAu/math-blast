🔑 Key Concept

When fractions have the same denominator, comparing and adding is straightforward — just work with the top numbers. Different denominators need converting first.

💬 Conversation Starters

  • After Q1–2: "If a pizza has 8 slices and you eat 3, have you eaten more or less than half? How do you know?"
  • After Q3–4: "If I give you 1/4 of my sandwich and 1/4 of dad's sandwich — would you get the same amount? Why not?"
  • After Q9–10 (boss): "Can you work backwards? If I have 2 whole tanks and I pour out 5/8 — how much is left? How do you convert to eighths?"
