Questions
FAQ
What age is it for?
Math Blast is designed for children aged 7–9 (Years 2–4). The maths content follows the Australian National Curriculum — times tables, fractions, geometry, measurement, and more.
Do we need to install anything?
No. Each issue is a webpage — just click the link. Works on any phone, tablet, or computer. No app, no account for your child, no passwords.
What's the difference between the adventure worlds?
Each world has a different hero, setting, and maths focus. The Space arc (Jake & Bolt) covers fractions and times tables. The Jungle arc (Blaze) focuses on measurement and patterns. Pyramids and Treasure Hunt are coming soon. A subscription gives access to all active worlds.
How long does each issue take?
About 15–25 minutes — perfect for a Sunday morning or after-school wind-down. There's audio narration too, so you can listen along together.
Is it actually curriculum-aligned?
Yes. Every puzzle maps to the Australian Curriculum for Years 2–4. It's not just maths-flavoured fun — it covers the content they need to know.
What's the difference between the three pricing options?
A Single Arc ($12.99) gives you all 7 issues of one adventure world — great if you want to try one topic. The Two Arc Bundle ($19.99) saves $6 and suits families with kids at different year levels. The Founder Pass ($44.99) is a one-time payment that unlocks every arc we ever release — Space, Jungle, Pyramids, Treasure Hunt, and anything we add in future. No subscription, no ongoing fees.
Can I cancel?
Absolutely. No lock-in, no fees. Just email us and we'll stop your subscription straight away. Arc purchases are yours to keep regardless.
