# The Crystal Compass — Issue 2: "The Forest of Patterns"

**MLC Value:** Wonder — patterns are everywhere  
**Y2 Maths:** Skip counting by 2s, 5s, and 10s  
**Chapters:** 5  
**Quiz questions:** 10  
**Audience:** Year 2 girls, ~7 years old  
**Writing rules:** Max 10–12 words per sentence. Simple words only.

---

## Characters This Issue
- **Priya** — leads this issue (Wonder is her value)
- **Isla, Zoe, Amara** — present throughout

## Chapter Breakdown

---

### Chapter 1 — Into the Forest

**Setting:** The glowing forest. Cyan and purple trees. Soft mist.

**Story:**
The girls step through the door.
The library disappears behind them.
All around them — glowing trees and floating lights.
The lights pulse slowly. On. Off. On. Off.
"It's beautiful," whispers Amara.
Priya stares at the lights. Something about them feels familiar.
The compass hums softly in Zoe's hand.
It's pointing deeper into the forest.

**Q1 — Warm Up:**
The lights flash in a pattern: 2, 4, 6, 8, __.
*What comes next?* **(10)**

*Why it fits:* Gentle entry — skip counting by 2s, the most familiar pattern for Y2.

**Image scene:** Four girls just inside the forest entrance, looking up in wonder at glowing cyan and purple trees. Floating lights pulse around them. Mist at their feet.

---

### Chapter 2 — Priya Sees It

**Setting:** Deeper in the forest. The lights pulse in groups.

**Story:**
Priya grabs Isla's arm. "Stop. Look at the lights."
They pulse in groups. Two at a time. Then five at a time.
"It's a pattern!" Priya says. "They're counting!"
Isla squints. "Counting what?"
"I don't know yet," says Priya. "But if we follow the pattern — maybe we find out."
She starts counting along with the lights.
The compass needle twitches. It's responding.

**Q2:**
The lights flash: 10, 12, 14, __, 18.
*What's missing?* **(16)**

**Q3:**
Now they flash in fives: 5, 10, 15, __, 25.
*What's missing?* **(20)**

**Q4:**
Another group: 30, 35, __, 45, 50.
*What's missing?* **(40)**

*Why it fits:* Moves from 2s to 5s. Each question has a missing number in the middle — slightly harder than "what comes next."

**Image scene:** Priya pointing excitedly at the pulsing lights, notebook open. The lights glow in groups. Other girls watching.

---

### Chapter 3 — The River

**Setting:** A wide glowing river blocks the path. Stepping stones stretch across.

**Story:**
The path ends at a glowing river.
Stepping stones cross to the other side.
Each stone has a number on it.
But some numbers are missing.
"We have to step on the right stones," says Zoe.
"In the right order."
"Or what?" asks Isla.
The water glows an ominous red below the wrong stones.
"Let's not find out," says Amara.

**Q5:**
The stones count by 10s: 10, 20, __, 40, 50.
*Which stone is missing?* **(30)**

**Q6:**
Next row: 40, 50, 60, __, 80.
*Which stone is missing?* **(70)**

**Q7:**
Final row counts by 2s: 22, 24, 26, __, 30.
*Which stone?* **(28)**

*Why it fits:* Stepping stones are a classic skip-counting visual. The stakes (red water) add excitement without being scary.

**Image scene:** The four girls at the edge of a glowing river. Stepping stones with numbers, some glowing gold (correct) and some dark. The water shimmers below.

---

### Chapter 4 — The Pattern Lock (⚡ SPEED ROUND)

**Setting:** A massive stone door at the end of the forest path — and the river behind them starts rising.

**Story:**
They make it across. The path leads to a huge stone door.
Carved into it is a row of circles — like a number line.
Isla tries to push the door. It doesn't move.
Then — a rumble. The river behind them is rising.
Water creeps up the bank toward their feet.
"The door needs patterns!" shouts Priya. "Fast!"

**⚡ SPEED ROUND — 5 questions, 8 seconds each**
*UI: rushing water sound effect, water level rising on screen as timer counts down*

**Q8:** 0, 10, 20, __, 40 — what's missing? **(30)**
**Q9:** 15, 20, 25, __ — what comes next? **(30)**
**Q10:** 6, 8, 10, __ — what comes next? **(12)**
**Q11:** 50, 60, __, 80 — what's missing? **(70)**
**Q12:** Count by 5s: 35, 40, __, 50 — what's missing? **(45)**

*Score shown after: "You answered X out of 5 before the water reached you!"*
*Story continues regardless of score.*

The door rumbles and swings open just in time.
They tumble through, laughing and breathless.

*Why it fits:* Rising water is visceral urgency — kids will feel it. Questions are pattern completion, same concept as the chapter, faster pace.

**Image scene:** Priya frantically tracing the door's circles. Behind them, glowing water rises up the bank. The door beginning to glow and open.

---

### Chapter 5 — Through the Door

**Setting:** Beyond the stone door — the base of a huge glowing mountain.

**Story:**
The door rumbles and swings open.
Cool mountain air rushes in.
The compass spins and points upward.
They look up. The mountain is enormous.
Stone steps wind all the way to the top.
Each step has a number carved into it — glowing faintly.
"We have to climb," says Isla. She's already moving.
Amara laughs. "Of course she is."

**Q10 — Final challenge:**
Count by 2s: start at 0, finish at 20.
*How many jumps?* **(10)**

*Why it fits:* Mirrors Q9 — counting the number of jumps, not just the values. Locks in the concept before the cliffhanger.

**Image scene (cliffhanger):** The girls at the base of a huge glowing mountain, looking up. Stone steps with faintly glowing numbers spiral upward into the clouds. Isla is already a step ahead.

## Parent Guide

**What your child learned:**
Skip counting — jumping forward by the same number each time. Counting by 2s, 5s, and 10s is a key building block for multiplication later on.

**Try this at home:**
Clap and count together by 2s to 20. Then try 5s to 50. Ask: "What pattern do you notice in the ones digit when we count by 5s?"
