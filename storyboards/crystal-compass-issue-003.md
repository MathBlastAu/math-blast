# The Crystal Compass — Issue 3: "The Courage Climb"

**MLC Value:** Courage — facing big challenges, helping others  
**Y2 Maths:** Addition and subtraction to 100  
**Chapters:** 5  
**Quiz questions:** 10  
**Audience:** Year 2 girls, ~7 years old  
**Writing rules:** Max 10–12 words per sentence. Simple words only.

## Characters This Issue
- **Isla** — leads this issue (Courage is her value)
- **Priya, Zoe, Amara** — present throughout

## Chapter Breakdown

---

### Chapter 1 — The Climb Begins

**Setting:** Base of the glowing mountain. Stone steps wind upward.

**Story:**
The steps go up and up and up.
Isla looks at the others. "I'll go first."
She steps onto the first stone.
It glows gold under her foot.
A number appears. Then another. Then a plus sign.
"It's a puzzle," she calls down.
"Solve it — and the step holds."
"Don't solve it..." She looks down. A long way down.
"Let's solve it," says Amara firmly.

**Q1 — Warm Up:**
The first step shows: 23 + 14 = ?
**(37)**

*Why it fits:* Simple two-digit addition, no regrouping. Gets the girls onto the mountain confidently.

**Image scene:** Isla stepping boldly onto the first stone step, which glows gold. Numbers appear on the stone. Girls behind her watching.

---

### Chapter 2 — Getting Harder

**Setting:** Halfway up the mountain. The steps are steeper.

**Story:**
They climb steadily. Each step lights up with a new problem.
Some are easy. Some make them stop and think.
Priya keeps a tally in her notebook.
"Seven steps solved," she says. "Good progress."
Isla doesn't slow down.
"Courage isn't just about being brave," Zoe says quietly.
"It's about keeping going when it gets hard."
Isla grins back at her. "Then let's keep going."

**Q2:**
45 + 30 = ? **(75)**

**Q3:**
61 + 18 = ? **(79)**

**Q4:**
50 − 20 = ? **(30)**

**Q5:**
78 − 15 = ? **(63)**

*Why it fits:* Mix of addition and subtraction. Q2 and Q3 have no regrouping. Q4 and Q5 introduce subtraction — still manageable.

**Image scene:** The four girls climbing the mountain steps together. Some steps glow gold (solved), some still dark ahead. The view behind them is spectacular.

---

### Chapter 3 — Amara Slips

**Setting:** Three-quarters up. A narrow ledge.

**Story:**
The path gets narrow.
Amara's foot slips on a wet stone.
She grabs the wall. Her heart is hammering.
Isla is already three steps ahead.
She stops. Turns around. Comes back.
She reaches out her hand.
"You've got this," Isla says. "I've got you."
Amara takes her hand. They climb the next step together.
The step glows twice as bright.

**Q6:**
34 + 46 = ? **(80)**

**Q7:**
90 − 27 = ? **(63)**

*Why it fits:* Q6 requires regrouping (4+6=10, carry the 1). Q7 is a harder subtraction. The emotional beat — Isla coming back for Amara — mirrors the MLC Courage value: courage isn't just personal bravery.

**Image scene:** Isla reaching her hand back to Amara on a narrow mountain ledge. The step they're standing on glows warmly. Priya and Zoe close behind.

---

### Chapter 4 — The Summit in Sight (⚡ SPEED ROUND)

**Setting:** Near the top — and the steps start crumbling beneath their feet.

**Story:**
They can see the top now.
Then — a crack. A step splits in two and tumbles down.
Then another. And another.
The steps are falling apart from the bottom up.
"RUN!" shouts Isla.
They sprint. Each step only holds for a moment.
Numbers flash on each stone — solve it or it crumbles.
No time. No hesitation. Just go.

**⚡ SPEED ROUND — 5 questions, 8 seconds each**
*UI: rumbling sound, stones falling away at the bottom of screen, timer counts down*

**Q8:** 55 + 25 = ? **(80)**
**Q9:** 100 − 38 = ? **(62)**
**Q10:** 40 + 40 = ? **(80)**
**Q11:** 73 − 20 = ? **(53)**
**Q12:** 36 + 44 = ? **(80)**

*Score shown after: "You answered X out of 5 before the steps fell!"*
*Story continues regardless of score.*

They leap onto the summit. The last step crumbles behind them.
They're breathing hard. They made it.

*Why it fits:* Crumbling steps is the most dramatic speed round of the arc — perfectly matches Isla's Courage value. Questions are harder addition/subtraction to match the chapter difficulty.

**Image scene:** The four girls sprinting up crumbling stone steps. Steps falling away behind them. Numbers flashing on each stone. Summit just ahead.

---

### Chapter 5 — The View from the Top

**Setting:** The mountain summit. Clear sky. Everything below looks tiny.

**Story:**
They step onto the top together.
The compass blazes gold — the brightest yet.
The view takes their breath away.
Mountains. Forest. The glowing library far below.
And then — they see something else.
Far down in the valley — a village.
But something is wrong.
The bridges look too short. The buildings look wonky.
The compass points straight down toward it.

**Q10 — Final challenge:**
47 + 36 = ? **(83)**

*Why it fits:* Requires regrouping — a satisfying capstone to the addition/subtraction arc. Then the cliffhanger reveal.

**Image scene (cliffhanger):** Four girls at the mountain summit, compass blazing gold. Far below in a valley — a magical village, but things are clearly the wrong size. The compass points downward.

## Parent Guide

**What your child learned:**
Adding and subtracting two-digit numbers — including when you need to regroup (carry). This is a big step in Year 2 and takes practice. The key idea: when ones add up to 10 or more, a ten "moves over" to the tens column.

**Try this at home:**
Use 10 small objects (coins, blocks). Practice making numbers: "Show me 34 — 3 groups of 10 and 4 leftover." Then add another small number. Can you regroup?
