🌳 Chapter 1 — A Forest That Shouldn't Be Disturbed

The Trail of Clues Leads Here...

Press ▶ to hear the story
Blaze had been following the trail of clues. Footprints in Branchwick. Wrong divisions in Fernmoss. Tangle learning grouping patterns at the Cascade Festival. Each one pointing deeper into the jungle — and now she was here.

The Array Forest was the most sacred site in the Verdant Canopy: trees planted in perfect rectangular rows and columns by ancient Sprocket mathematicians. But someone had moved through it. Rows were broken. Trees were missing from their positions.

A small Sprocket named Keeper Moss met her at the entrance, clutching a scroll of records. "If we cannot verify the arrays, our entire history is lost. Twenty-four trees — four equal rows. Can you tell me how many per row?"

🔍 Chapter 2 — Row by Row

Twelve Again. Always Twelve.

Press ▶ to hear the story
Blaze worked through the forest with Keeper Moss, checking each array against the ancient records. Every tree in its place — or it wasn't.

The second array: thirty-five trees in five equal rows. The third: forty-eight trees in six equal rows. As she worked, Blaze kept noticing something. Vine bundles, left behind. Always in groups of twelve.

"Twelve again," she murmured. "Whatever has been through here — it's left its signature."

🏛️ Chapter 3 — The Prestige Plantings

The Oldest Arrays in the Forest!

Press ▶ to hear the story
At the heart of the forest stood the prestige arrays — the oldest and most precise plantings of all. These were the ones Keeper Moss feared most for.

The first prestige array: ninety-six trees in eight rows. The second: eighty-four trees in seven rows. Keeper Moss watched anxiously as Blaze worked through the calculations.

"Both come out the same," Blaze said, surprised. "Twelve per row each time. That can't be a coincidence."

🌀 Chapter 4 — Tangle's Array

This Wasn't Damage. This Was Creation.

And then Blaze saw it. Not a tree array — a vine array. One hundred and thirty-two vines, arranged in eleven neat bundles. Twelve per bundle. Perfectly formed.

She crouched down slowly. This was not damage. This was creation. Whatever had been moving through the Array Forest wasn't disturbing it — it was adding to it.

At the far edge of the clearing, something enormous shifted in the shadows. Blaze stayed very still.

🤝 Chapter 5 — The Gift

Blaze Reaches Out Her Hand.

Press ▶ to hear the story
Keeper Moss was ready to declare the records restored. But Blaze hadn't moved. She was still watching the edge of the clearing.

Then she did something that surprised even herself. She reached out her hand.

The enormous shape didn't run. Slowly — very slowly — it moved forward. And then it set something down at her feet. A bundle of twelve vines, perfectly tied.

"It's been trying to help," Blaze said softly. "This whole time. It just didn't know how."
