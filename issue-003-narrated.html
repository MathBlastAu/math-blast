🔑 Key Concept

The Phantom's mistake: it believes taking the same FRACTION from everyone is fair. But 1/2 of a large tank ≠ 1/2 of a small tank in real terms. Same fraction, different whole = different real amount.

💬 Conversation Starters

  • After Q1–2: "Can you show me two different ways to write one half? What about one third? Try folding a piece of paper to check."
  • After Q4: "If 3/4 of a pizza = 6/8 of a pizza, why do they look like different numbers? What happens when you cut the slices differently?"
  • After Q8–10 (boss): "If I gave you half of my pocket money and half of Dad's pocket money, would you get the same amount? Why or why not?"
