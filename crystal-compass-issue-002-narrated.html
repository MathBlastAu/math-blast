What You Mastered Today 🧠

  • ✅ Skip counting by 2s — jumping forward in twos all the way to 30!
  • ✅ Skip counting by 5s — five at a time, spotting the pattern
  • ✅ Skip counting by 10s — the fastest way to count big numbers
  • ✅ Missing numbers — finding the gap in the middle of a sequence
  • ✅ Counting jumps — how many steps to get from 0 to 20 by 2s

🏠 Parent Guide

What your child learned: Skip counting — jumping forward by the same number each time. Counting by 2s, 5s, and 10s is a key building block for multiplication later on.

Try this at home: Clap and count together by 2s to 20. Then try 5s to 50. Ask: "What pattern do you notice in the ones digit when we count by 5s?"
