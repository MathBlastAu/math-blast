The compass blazed gold. The needle locked in place. It pointed straight at the far wall.

🏠 Parent Guide

What your child learned: Place value — the idea that where a digit sits changes its value completely. The 4 in 47 means 4 tens (forty). The 4 in 74 means 4 ones. This is one of the most important ideas in Year 2 maths.

Try this at home: Write 36 on paper. Ask "How many tens? How many ones?" Then swap the digits to make 63. Ask "Is this bigger or smaller? Why?"
