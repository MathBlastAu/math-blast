🔑 Key Insight

The Boss question reveals the Phantom's flaw: taking the same fraction from different-sized tanks means different real amounts lost. 1/2 of 16 ≠ 1/2 of 8 in actual units.

💬 Conversation Starters

  • After Q1–3: "Fold a piece of paper in half, then in half again. Can you see how 1/2 = 2/4?"
  • After Q5: "Draw a number line from 0 to 1. Can you place 1/2, 2/4 and 4/8 — do they land in the same spot?"
  • After Q10 (boss): "If we both save half our pocket money this week but yours is $10 and mine is $20, do we save the same amount?"
