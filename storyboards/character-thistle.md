# CHARACTER PROFILE — THISTLE
## *Math Blast* Jungle Arc — Fernmoss Village

---

## OVERVIEW

**Name:** Thistle  
**Role:** Supporting character, Issues 2–3 (Fernmoss)  
**Species:** Sprocket — Fernmoss variety (slightly darker blue than Branchwick Sprockets, with bioluminescent spots that glow faintly in the mushroom light)  
**Personality:** Earnest, anxious, deeply conscientious. Thistle is Fernmoss's acting medicine keeper — a role they were not prepared for, because the real keeper got cut off when Tangle took the vines. Thistle has been trying to do the right thing with the wrong numbers, and feels terrible about it.

## CHARACTER NOTES

- Thistle is not comic relief — they're sympathetic. The wrong divisions weren't laziness, they were trying their best with incomplete information.
- When Blaze corrects them, Thistle doesn't get defensive — they're relieved someone knows what to do.
- Distinct from Doz: where Doz is chaotic and excitable, Thistle is quiet and worried.
- Distinct from Pip: where Pip is authoritative and ceremonial, Thistle is uncertain and humble.
