🌍 Arc Resolution

This finale ties together every concept from Issues 1–6. The Phantom's error — taking equal fractions from unequal wholes — is the thread running through the whole story.

🔑 The Big Idea

Fairness requires understanding the WHOLE, not just the fraction. 1/4 of 40 ≠ 1/4 of 16. This is a profound mathematical truth kids carry for life.

💬 Conversation Starters

  • After Q1: "If we shared half our pizza with the neighbours AND half our little personal pizza, does each person get the same amount? Why not?"
  • After Q7: "The car is half full of petrol. If we need it 3/4 full for the trip, how much do we add? (Try with your actual tank size!)"
  • After Q10 (boss): "The Phantom needed 30 units but had 60 — what would you do with the spare 30?"
