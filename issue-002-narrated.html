🔑 Key Concept

Q10 introduces fractions of fractions — a powerful idea. 1/4 of 1/3 means dividing a third into four equal parts. The result (1/12) is smaller than either fraction alone.

💬 Conversation Starters

  • After Q4–5: "If we had 12 biscuits and shared half with the neighbours, how many would we keep? Then if we put a third of ours aside for tomorrow — how many is that?"
  • After Q6: "What's bigger — 2/3 of a pizza or 3/4? How could you check with a piece of paper?"
  • After Q10 (boss): "Can you draw a rectangle and show me what 1/12 looks like? How did you get there?"
