🔑 Key Insight

When you take the same FRACTION from tanks of different sizes, the absolute amounts are different. This is the Phantom's fundamental error — and Issue #7 resolves it.

💬 Conversation Starters

  • After Q2–3: "Can you think of another mixed number and convert it to an improper fraction? Try 2 and 1/2."
  • After Q4–5: "The Phantom took 1/3 from each ship — but one ship lost 8 units and another lost 6. Is that fair? Why or why not?"
  • After Q10 (boss): "What would happen if the Phantom took 1/4 from ships of 8, 12, and 20 units? Would that be fair?"
