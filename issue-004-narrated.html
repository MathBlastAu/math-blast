🔑 Key Insight

4/4 = 1 is a profound idea: whole numbers are fractions too. Every whole number can be written as a fraction (1 = 2/2 = 4/4 = 8/8). This bridges the number line.

💬 Conversation Starters

  • After Q4: "Is 2/3 or 3/4 of a pizza bigger? How could we check by folding paper?"
  • After Q6–7: "If 4/4 = 1, what does 8/4 equal? What about 12/4? Can you keep going?"
  • After Q10 (boss): "Why do you think the Phantom thought 4/4 wasn't a fraction? Was it partly right?"
